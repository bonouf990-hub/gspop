# AMFM — Implementation Hint Points (source requirements)

The original client requirements document for the ARENCO facilities platform.
This is an **Asset & Maintenance / Facilities Management (AMFM)** system, centered
on the asset hierarchy **Building > Floor > Apartment > Equipment**. 17 modules.

## 1. Asset Management
- Centralized Asset Register
- Asset hierarchy (Building > Floor > Apartment > Equipment)
- Asset tagging (QR code / Barcode / RFID)
- Asset lifecycle management
- Warranty tracking
- Asset history and maintenance records
- Asset condition assessments

## 2. Preventive Maintenance (PPM)
- Maintenance scheduling (daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, annually)
- Automatic PPM work order generation
- Preventive maintenance checklists
- Maintenance calendar
- Compliance-based maintenance
- Missed maintenance alerts, Notification & Escalation

## 3. Corrective Maintenance
- Resident maintenance requests
- Work order creation and tracking
- Priority classification (Emergency, Urgent, Routine)
- Technician assignment
- Escalation procedures
- Repair history
- Root cause analysis

## 4. Work Order Management
- Digital work orders
- Mobile work order management
- Labor and material tracking
- Photo attachments (before and after)
- Completion verification
- Resident feedback on completed work
- SLA monitoring

## 5. Resident Portal
- Submit maintenance requests
- Track request status / on-time update
- Service feedback

## 6. Mobile Application
- Technician mobile app
- Resident mobile app
- Real-time notifications
- QR code scanning
- GPS-enabled attendance
- Offline work capability
- Digital signatures

## 7. Help Desk
- 24/7 complaint logging
- Multi-channel request intake (App, Web, Phone, Email)
- Ticket management
- Escalation matrix
- Automatic notifications
- Service request tracking

## 8. Asset Inspections (Common Area)
- Inspection scheduling
- Digital inspection forms
- Compliance checklists
- Defect recording
- Inspection reports & update with photos

## 9. Inventory and Spare Parts
- Spare parts inventory
- Minimum stock levels (View Only)
- Material Requisition
- Material issue tracking
- Purchase request integration with JD (external ERP)

## 10. Vendor and Contractor Management
- Vendor database
- Service contracts
- Performance evaluation
- Contractor attendance / service due notification

## 11. Contract Management
- AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) management
- Contract renewal reminders
- Scope tracking
- Service performance
- Payment milestones

## 12. Building Systems Monitoring
- HVAC, Electrical, Plumbing, Fire alarm, Firefighting systems
- Elevators, Water tanks, Pumps, Generator sets
- Building Management System (BMS) integration

## 13. Utility Management
- Electricity, Water, Gas monitoring
- Utility billing, Meter readings
- Consumption trends, Cost analysis

## 14. Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE)
- Incident reporting
- Permit-to-work system

## 15. Compliance Management (Assets & Common Area)
- Civil Defense agreement & safety compliance certificate
- Lift inspections
- DM (Dubai Municipality) related reports

## 16. Dashboard and Reporting
- Asset performance, Work order status, PPM compliance
- Maintenance costs vs monthly budget
- Utility consumption, Technician productivity & performance evaluation
- SLA performance, Vendor performance, KPI dashboard

## 17. Document Management
- Drawings, O&M manuals, Warranties
- Inspection certificates, Equipment manuals
- Contracts, Safety documents
